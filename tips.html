Essential LGBTQI+ Travel Tips

Traveling as an LGBTQI+ individual can be an empowering experience when you're informed, prepared, and confident. Here are our top tips to help you slay safely around the world.

🌍 Know the Local Laws

Always research the destination's laws and attitudes toward LGBTQI+ people. Some countries still criminalize same-sex relationships, while others celebrate them openly.

👯 Travel with Community

If you're unsure about a destination, consider group travel or guided LGBTQI+ tours. You'll feel safer and make fabulous new friends along the way.

🧠 Stay Informed & Respectful

Culture matters! Respect local customs while still being your authentic self. Sometimes blending in can mean staying safe — other times, it’s about standing proud.
